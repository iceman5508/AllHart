Pastor Kennith Flowers Jr.

Pastor Ken A. Flowers Jr. is a second generation minister, who received Jesus Christ as his Lord and savior at the age of fourteen in the city of Plainfield. He preached his first revival at sixteen in Newark NJ. He served as the co- pastor of Living Water Ministries in Plainfield for 9 years. Pastor is marred to Lady Renee Flowers for fourteen years they have one daughter. He being radically saved at a young age, has kept a fire within him to see young people, teenagers be changed by an encounter with JESUS. His continuous prayer as a watchman on the wall is to see Jesus Prayer before he was arrested be manifested in the body of CHRIST TODAY in every town of every state in America. UNITY. Through prayer and supplication and working together (functional unity)in our cities to uproot everything that is against FATHER GOD AND THE CHRIST. He has also the burden passed down to him by his father Bishop Ken A. Flowers Sr. to THE GLORY OF GOD breakout in revival, great awakening power in the east coast region, and all through the United States of America.

Frequently Ask Questions

What is your beliefs?

  • We belive that JESUS is the only way to GOD. (14:6)
  • We believe that GOD THE FATHER, THE SON, THE HOY SPIRIT ARE ONE. (john 1:12, John 17, 1John 5)
  • We believe that the bible is the inspired word Of GOD, written by men under the direction of the HOLY SPIRIT.
  • We believe the bible and all of its truths were canonized to bring the human race back unto truth, ultimately back to GOD through JESUS CHRIST.
  • We believe in the practice of tithes and offerings. Seeing that tithing was done even before the LAW OF MOSES. We perceive it as an act of worship and love to GOD, Not something done out of fear.(Genesis 14:20)
  • We believe that signs and wonders, healings, are still manifested today by the will of the father.
  • whether he works through man or by his sovereign hand.
  • We believe in ISRAEL as a sovereign state. She has the right to the land she is on given to her by GOD THE FATHER. We believe Israel has the right to defend herself against all enemies, terroristic threats that would like to remove her from the promised land.
  • We believe that Israel and the church(gentile non-jewish believers are united as one new man for Kingdom of GOD purposes in the earth.(Ephesians 2:11-22)
  • We affirm that the earth is in the last days according to the scriptures. We are affirm the earth is experiencing tribulation. But has yet to undergo the GREAT TRIUBLATION.
  • We affirm the Church will not on earth during the GREAT TRIBULATION but will return with JESUS and thousands of his saints to the Mount of Olives.
